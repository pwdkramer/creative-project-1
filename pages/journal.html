Field Journal

  • My New Assignment ---6 days ago
    I received a new assignment from the Jedi Counsil today.
    They asked me to travel to Dantooine and investigate rumors of some uncovered Jedi Ruins.
    I'm not sure what there is to learn from some old fossils, but my master assures me that the Force lingers in these ancient places.
    I hope to learn something of value out there.

  • In Transit ---5 days ago
    I'm traveling to Dantooine on a public trasport shuttle, it should take about 3 days to arrive.
    I'm told to meet a guide for the planet when I arrive, but I have yet to learn the name of this individual.
    The other passengers have taken to playing games of Sabaak to pass the time, I'm unsure whether or not the order would frown upon me joining.
    I suppose I'll have time to meditate and hope to find some clarity in what I am to do once I arrive.

  • Dantooine ---2 days ago
    Well, I have finally arrived at the Dantooine space port.
    They say there used to be a Jedi Temple here long ago, during the times of the Old Republic.
    I have yet to meet my guide for this place, and find myself wandering the outskirts of the port and waiting in the Cantina for something, anything to happen.
    I have heard rumors of some old caves a few clicks out with some strange stories surrounding them, I can't help but feel a pull from the Force in their direction.

  • The Caves ---1 day ago
    I set off this morning in search of these caves I've heard so much about, I can't believe what I've found here!
    The Force is strong in this place, and I've even found pockets of what I believe to be Kaiber Crystals!
    There are parts deep in the cave though that trouble me, I feel a pull towards them but as I ventured in that direction, I could begin to feel the caves resonate with the power of the Dark Side.
    How could there be such a presence in a place that has such affinity with the light???
  • Folly ---1 hour ago
    Despite all my training and the warning of the Dark Side given to me time and time again by my masters, I couln't resist the pull coming from the deeper parts of the cave...
    I stumbled upon an ancient structure, and what I believe to be a map, but there was an ancient Sith security system and now I find myself trapped.
    I fear this cave will become my tomb... But there is such knowledge here trapped with me!
    I only hope that my absence will cause the Order to come in search of me, and even if they don't arrive before I become one with the Force, they will find a way to use the knowledge here.
